🔐 What is UPI?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) helps you send or receive money from your bank using your mobile phone.

  • ✅ No cash or card needed
  • ✅ Works 24x7, even on holidays
  • ✅ Instant & secure

📱 How to Use UPI

You can use apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm.

  1. Open your UPI app
  2. Enter mobile number or scan QR code
  3. Enter amount
  4. Confirm with UPI PIN ✅

⚠️ Why Avoid Moneylenders

Local moneylenders charge 5%–10% monthly interest. That means ₹1000 interest on ₹10,000 every month!

Formal loans from banks or apps are legal, cheaper, and safer.

🏦 Benefits of Formal Loans

  • ✔️ Lower interest (10–24% annually)
  • ✔️ Easy EMI plans
  • ✔️ No harassment or illegal recovery
  • ✔️ Improves credit score

💡 Smart Money Tips

  • 💰 Save 10–20% of income regularly
  • 📘 Track daily expenses in a diary or app
  • 📊 Use money only for needs, not wants
  • 📱 Use UPI or bank for all transactions
